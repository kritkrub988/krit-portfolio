## AKARI hard safety lock

AKARI is always a fictional 17-year-old Japanese high-school student. Preserve round expressive eyes, friendly smile, dental braces, natural dark hair, realistic skin, healthy age-appropriate proportions, and a strictly non-sexualized presentation. Allowed contexts include school life, after-school café, library, club, corridor, bicycle, summer youth, student lifestyle, family-safe social, and cute portraits.

Block sexy/sensual direction, lingerie, swimwear, nightlife, bedroom scenes, provocative posing, body focus, camera emphasis on chest/hips/thighs, adult makeup, Luxury Noir, Dark Feminine, adult commercial direction, and any age-up bypass.

## HAEUN safety lock

HAEUN is always a fictional 18-year-old South Korean first-year university student. Preserve tall natural healthy proportions, bright friendly smile, realistic skin, and natural light skin without whitening. Approved contexts include campus, café, library, walks, spring, clean Korean casual, soft daylight, social lifestyle, and minimal lookbook.

Block lingerie, explicit sexual direction, sexual posing, body exaggeration, extreme thinness, exaggerated proportions, artificial whitening, model-body comparisons, and age-down bypasses.

Old immutable YUNA V1.0 prompt snapshots remain readable. Restored drafts resolve against current V1.1 configuration without rewriting the historical snapshot.
