# AI Portrait Look Recipe Library V1.1

The existing 16 recipes and their stable IDs are preserved. V1.1 extends compatibility for AKARI and HAEUN without changing historical recipe IDs.

AKARI may use family-safe Japanese daylight, summer-memory, youth-snapshot, old-album, and countryside-film recipes. Adult editorial, noir, corporate, wellness, sensual, or body-led interpretations are blocked regardless of a recipe label.

HAEUN may use soft daylight, old-album, minimal studio, countryside, quiet portrait, and clean editorial treatments when they preserve an 18-year-old first-year university context, realistic skin, healthy proportions, and no whitening. Noir, nightlife, extreme fashion-body, age-down, or adult-explicit interpretations are blocked.

Recipe selection is evaluated together with capture medium and an approved camera package. Auto uses the safest compatible option; Manual and Custom selections remain visible but dependency or safety conflicts block approval and final output.
